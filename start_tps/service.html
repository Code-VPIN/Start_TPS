Analytics

Data analytics is a broad term that encompasses many diverse types of data analysis. Any type of information can be subjected to data analytics techniques to get insight that can be used to improve things. Data analytics techniques can reveal trends and metrics that would otherwise be lost in the mass of information. This information can then be used to optimize processes to increase the overall efficiency of a business or system.​

Digital Marketing

Any online advertising, including online content marketing, is called digital marketing. SEO, pay-per-click advertising, email marketing, video marketing, and other forms of digital marketing are examples. When considering the difference between digital marketing vs content marketing, one has to understand that not all digital marketing is content marketing, but all content marketing is digital marketing. ​

Custom Integration

Custom integrations enable a business to integrate their software, systems, platforms, or websites with each other according to their business's unique needs and specifications. While certain generic integration processes are already available and mapped out for big software brands like Microsoft, specialized integrations allow businesses to join their software together in a way that works best for their individual needs. Custom integrations can help automate processes and improve your company's overall efficiency.​
